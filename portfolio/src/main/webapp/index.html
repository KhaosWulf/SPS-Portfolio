Hello! As you can see, this is my portfolio; a small but rather in-depth look into what makes me, me. Nice of you to stop by!

But I enjoy it! A nice little love-hate relationship; healthy for any aspiring computer scientist, really. All the more motivation to work towards mastering more skills and getting out of here and into my desired purpose. For instance, I have a hefty interest in robotics, both the software and hardware side. As for what I enjoy doing when not focusing so intensly on any of that, I am an avid creative writer, and artist. I do so greatly love reimagining and crafting different realities, people, and situations that all come together to create such entracing, intricate stories. I enjoy role-playing games (RPGs) and historically-inspired games as well, such as Assassin's Creed and some Call of Duty games. That is just timeless.

Lastly, I am quiet personality, but so love working with people to hear the unique perspectives unique individual's provide. Each person shapes their own reality, always ever slightly different from the next. Such diversity helps in innovation and design, and I welcome such a benefit!
